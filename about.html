About

Trial Law Firm actively promotes ease of business in Ghana by offering top-notch, user-friendly legal advice to both domestic and international investors. We take on the responsibility of guiding our clients through the legal and regulatory landscape, providing comprehensive assistance and consultancy for international organizations on the Ghanaian investment scheme. Our team has extensive experience in helping companies establish and operate successfully in Ghana, covering business formation and financing, operational matters, as well as various corporate transactions, contracts, securities, joint ventures, shareholding agreements, corporate governance, and company secretarial services. Our lawyers are committed to delivering cutting-edge legal services by thoroughly researching, anticipating, and mitigating potential risks and problems. We find solutions to all challenges, whether simple or complex.

Our team of experienced lawyers specializes in providing legal advisory services, navigating corporate and commercial transactions, and performing regulatory compliance and research work across various sectors, including Project Development and Finance, Infrastructure and Construction, Energy, and Natural Resources. We have a team of lawyers trained and certified in FIDIC construction contracts. The firm’s Corporate/Commercial department possesses extensive experience in corporate governance and internal compliance monitoring, adhering to both local and international laws such as the UK Bribery Act and the USA Foreign Corrupt Practices Act.

With excellence as our benchmark for service delivery, our team stays well-informed of global economic trends and forecasts in this ever-evolving marketplace. Comprising cross-border experienced lawyers with quality institutional training and a commitment to continuous learning, our team is multilingual and licensed to practice in Ghana, England and Wales, New York, and Nigeria. Our extensive knowledge across various sectors and jurisdictions enables us to provide insightful advice that meets our clients' business needs.
